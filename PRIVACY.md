# 隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

**最后更新日期 / Last Updated: 2025-01-01**

## 中文版本

### 1. 简介

Easy New Tab（以下简称"本扩展"）尊重并保护用户的隐私。本隐私政策说明了本扩展如何处理您的信息。

### 2. 信息收集

**本扩展不收集任何个人信息。**

本扩展使用的所有数据均存储在您的本地浏览器中，包括但不限于：
- 您添加的网站快捷方式（名称、URL、图标、颜色）
- 您创建的文件夹及其内容
- 您选择的背景图片 URL
- 您的搜索引擎偏好设置

### 3. 数据存储

- 所有数据通过浏览器的 `chrome.storage.local` API 存储在您的本地设备上
- 数据不会被上传到任何远程服务器
- 数据不会与任何第三方共享
- 您可以随时通过删除扩展来清除所有数据

### 4. 权限使用说明

本扩展仅请求以下权限：

- **storage**: 用于在您的浏览器本地存储您的自定义配置，使您的设置在浏览器重启后仍然保留

本扩展不会：
- 访问您的浏览历史记录
- 读取或修改您访问的网页内容
- 收集您的个人身份信息
- 追踪您的浏览行为
- 发送任何数据到外部服务器

### 5. 外部资源

本扩展可能使用以下外部资源：

- **网站图标（Favicon）**: 当您选择使用网站真实 Logo 时，扩展会从公共 favicon 服务（如 Google、Favicon.im）获取网站图标。这些请求不包含任何个人信息。
- **背景图片**: 您可以设置自定义背景图片 URL。扩展会从您提供的 URL 加载图片。

### 6. 第三方服务

本扩展本身不集成任何第三方分析、广告或追踪服务。

当您使用搜索功能时，您的搜索查询会被发送到您选择的搜索引擎（Google、Bing、百度或 DuckDuckGo）。这些搜索引擎有各自的隐私政策，请参考它们的官方文档。

### 7. 数据安全

由于所有数据都存储在您的本地浏览器中，数据的安全性取决于您的设备和浏览器的安全性。建议您：
- 保持浏览器和操作系统更新
- 使用强密码保护您的设备
- 不在公共或共享设备上使用敏感配置

### 8. 儿童隐私

本扩展不针对 13 岁以下的儿童，也不会故意收集儿童的个人信息。

### 9. 开源透明

本扩展是开源项目，源代码完全公开透明。您可以在 GitHub 上查看和审查所有代码：
https://github.com/[your-username]/easy-new-tab

### 10. 隐私政策变更

我们可能会不定期更新本隐私政策。任何变更都会在此页面上发布。

### 11. 联系我们

如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请通过以下方式联系我们：
- GitHub Issues: https://github.com/[your-username]/easy-new-tab/issues

## English Version

### 1. Introduction

Easy New Tab (hereinafter referred to as "the Extension") respects and protects user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Extension handles your information.

### 2. Information Collection

**The Extension does not collect any personal information.**

All data used by the Extension is stored locally in your browser, including but not limited to:
- Website shortcuts you add (names, URLs, icons, colors)
- Folders you create and their contents
- Your custom background image URL
- Your search engine preferences

### 3. Data Storage

- All data is stored locally on your device through the browser's `chrome.storage.local` API
- Data is not uploaded to any remote servers
- Data is not shared with any third parties
- You can clear all data at any time by removing the Extension

### 4. Permission Usage

The Extension only requests the following permission:

- **storage**: Used to store your custom configurations locally in your browser, so your settings persist after browser restarts

The Extension does NOT:
- Access your browsing history
- Read or modify content of web pages you visit
- Collect your personal identification information
- Track your browsing behavior
- Send any data to external servers

### 5. External Resources

The Extension may use the following external resources:

- **Website Favicons**: When you choose to use a website's real logo, the Extension fetches website icons from public favicon services (such as Google, Favicon.im). These requests do not contain any personal information.
- **Background Images**: You can set custom background image URLs. The Extension loads images from the URLs you provide.

### 6. Third-Party Services

The Extension itself does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

When you use the search feature, your search queries are sent to your chosen search engine (Google, Bing, Baidu, or DuckDuckGo). These search engines have their own privacy policies; please refer to their official documentation.

### 7. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally in your browser, data security depends on your device and browser security. We recommend that you:
- Keep your browser and operating system updated
- Use strong passwords to protect your device
- Avoid using sensitive configurations on public or shared devices

### 8. Children's Privacy

The Extension is not directed at children under 13 years of age and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

### 9. Open Source Transparency

The Extension is an open-source project with fully transparent source code. You can view and review all code on GitHub:
https://github.com/[your-username]/easy-new-tab

### 10. Privacy Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

### 11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through:
- GitHub Issues: https://github.com/[your-username]/easy-new-tab/issues
